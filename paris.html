Paris

Paris is known for its museums and architectural landmarks, the Louvre was the most visited art museum in the world in 2019 with 262 million visitors.The city hosted the Olympic games in 1900,1924 and will host the 2024 Summer Olympics.

Tourist attractions

  • Place de la Concorde
  • Seine River Cruises
  • Luxemboug Gardens
