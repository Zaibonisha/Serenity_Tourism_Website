Istanbul

Founded as Byzantion by Megarian colonists in 660 BC and renamed Constantinople in 330 CE , the city grew in size and influence becoming a beacon of Silk Road and one of the most important cities in history. It served as an imperial capital for almost sixteen centuries.

Tourist attractions

  • Topkapi Palace
  • Aya Sofya
  • Istanbul Archaeology Museum
  • Grand Bazaar
  • Spice Stall Spice Bazaar
